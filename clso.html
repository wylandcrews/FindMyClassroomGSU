Classroom South (CLSO)

The Classroom South Building can be found near many other main university buildings, like the Library and Langdale Hall. All but one of the building's main entrances are at street level, with the exception connected to the Library. The main entrance pictured above guides traffic through a study area, with classrooms and elevators found to the left of the entrance. Classrooms can be found on this floor and the floors above.
